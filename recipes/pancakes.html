Golgen Pancakes


Description

When it comes to iconic breakfast and brunch dishes, pancakes are right up there at the top of the list.
Nothing beats a fluffy, golden stack of homemade pancakes piled high, drenched in a waterfall of maple syrup.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 Tbsp + 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/4 cup Canola oil
  • 1 1/2 cups half & half
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
